15 Unique Places To Shop For Gifts In and Around Pittsburgh
Looking for some unique gift ideas? Check out these Pittsburgh based gems.
The holiday season is in full effect and we want to help your gift giving and buying a little easier. As you are crossing names off your list and trying to shop local as much as possible, here are 15 one-of-a-kind gems in Pittsburgh including boutiques, shops, galleries and more.
Felt Happiness
Milliner Juliane Gorman creates wearable art hats that are useful, comfortable and flattering. Once upon a time, hats were worn because of custom. Now, you make your own traditions. You don‚Äôt need a wedding or a derby as an excuse to wear a hat. All of her handcrafted, felted hats are made in Gibsonia, Pennsylvania. Wear a vibrant, sculptural hat and tap into your inner beauty.

Gallery on Penn
5935-5939 Penn Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15206
Gallery on Penn is a retail incubator space created to support local, minority business owners with a chance to test their products and the market. You can find anything from art, lingerie, accessories, to decorative items and apparel.

A post shared by Pittsburgh City Paper (@pghcitypaper)
Third Day Soaps
Third Day Soaps Garden is a holistic skin and body care company founded by Cynthia Hill. You can purchase therapeutic herbal bath and body products, soaps, lotions, creams, body scrubs, facial products for men and women, herbal teas, and more.

5450 Penn Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15206

Based in Pittsburgh and born in the city's skateboarding, graffiti, and hip-hop scenes, they represent a new generation of hustlers and creatives from all walks of life and have great street wear options.

Refresh PGH is Pittsburgh's 1st Sneaker Consignment/Buy & Sell Boutique offering a wide selection of rare sneakers, sneaker products, clothing and apparel.

Style Exchange Boutique
4211 Saw Mill Run Blvd, Pittsburgh, PA 15227
If you hate paying full price and love a designer deal on a dollar, you must check out Style Exchange Boutique. From J Crew to Jimmy Choo, special occasion gowns to Louis Vuitton and MCM handbags, experience the thrill of the find at a fraction of retail prices every day!

Stop in for great coffee, then walk around this former bank converted into retail featuring local artists and vendors! Find Blakley Jean jewelry, Two Faced Ceramics, Pittsburgh-themed stickers from Emily M, Authentically African by Moa and tons more in this amazing space full of great shopping.

Mello and Sons Vintage
Denim hunters, this is the stop for you. Vintage Levis, jackets, cutoffs, tees and much more in this Lawrenceville shop. Plus, you're going to love the friendly vibes here as the friendly owner shows you the wares and spins vinyl at the same time.

Soil Sisters
In Allentown, woman- and Black-owned Soil Sisters is a small business that has made a big impact to gardens throughout the city and suburbs. Not only are the seedlings and houseplants you go home with tended to with the utmost care, the friendly owners are happy to share their tips to ensure your growing garden is a success.

Kiya Tomlin
Clothes made for real women who live real lives and have real bodies. That's the promise from Kiya Tomlin, a fashion designer with a self-named store in Etna. And, if you're shopping for an NFL fan, Tomlin (notice the name? Her husband is Steelers coach Mike Tomlin) has exclusive collaborations with 22+ teams including the Pittsburgh Steelers for leggings, tops, shorts, and more you won't find anywhere else.

Kards Unlimited
When you need the perfect card to tie your present together, head to Kards Unlimited in Shadyside. This truly one-of-a-kind store is absolutely crammed with the most unique greeting cards you're going to find in the city, but is also the perfect gift shop full of ephemera including novelty mugs, pins, books, puzzles and Pittsburgh-based gifts sure to make everyone on your list jolly.

Paititi Inti
Imported gifts from Peru fill this small Squirrel Shop from floor to ceiling. One of the kindest shop owners anywhere is happy to help you browse clothing, stones, jewelry, pottery and other handmade goods and leave with the perfect gifts.
