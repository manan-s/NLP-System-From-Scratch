Top 10 RV Parks Near Pittsburgh
Start your engine and hit the road for an RV adventure in Pittsburgh’s backyard!
Discover the perfect blend of adventure and relaxation at the top RV parks near Pittsburgh. These RV campgrounds provide a serene escape to nature while offering convenient proximity to the city's
top attractions
, ensuring you can seamlessly transition from a day filled with
outdoor activities
to an evening spent exploring vibrant
restaurants
and lively
nightlife
.
1. Bear Run Campground
Address:
184 Badger Hill Rd. Portersville, PA 16101
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~35 miles
Just a few minutes from
Moraine State Park
and 30 minutes to Pittsburgh,
Bear Run Campground
is the perfect weekend getaway for families. With a Pirate Ship Playscape, playground, heated pool, and a plethora of outdoor activities, including kayaking and fishing, Bear Run Campground transforms every weekend into a memorable
family adventure
.
View this post on Instagram
A post shared by Bear Run Campground (@bearruncampground1975)
2. Rose Point Park - Cabins & Camping
Address:
8775 Old US 422, New Castle, PA 16101
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~45 miles
Rose Point Park
offers diverse campsites for both tents and RVs, each equipped with a fire ring and picnic table, and a camp calendar packed with family-friendly events, including a Fishing Derby, Wild West Chuck Wagon and Hawaiian Luau Weekends. Conveniently located near
McConnells Mill State Park
, campers enjoy easy access to picturesque hiking
trails
along
Slippery Rock Creek
.
View this post on Instagram
A post shared by Rose Point Park Cabins & Camping (@rosepointpark)
3. Yogi Bear’s Jellystone Park at Kozy Rest
Address:
449 Campground Rd. Harrisville, PA 16038
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~50 miles
Yogi Bear's Jellystone Park
is a versatile and family-friendly campground offering over 15 accommodation types including RV sites, yurts, and cabins. With a plethora of attractions, including laser tag, gem mining, mini golf, arts and crafts, and a pool, the park is the perfect getaway for families. During your stay take the one hour drive to Pittsburgh and make stops at
Carnegie Science Center
and the
National Aviary
to enjoy even more family-friendly activities.
View this post on Instagram
A post shared by Jellystone Park at Kozy Rest (@jellystoneparkatkozyrest)
4. Mountain Top RV Campground
Address:
873 Sun Mine Rd, Tarentum, PA 15084
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~20 miles
Enjoy the beauty of
Mountain Top RV Campground
, offering cabin rentals and spacious RV spots with picturesque views, picnic tables, and fire rings. Located just 25 minutes from
PNC Park
, this campsite provides the perfect blend of relaxation in nature and the opportunity to catch an exciting
Pirates
game.
5. Indian Brave Campground
Address:
159 Perry Hwy, Harmony, Pennsylvania 16037
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~25 miles
Unwind at
Indian Brave Campground
with versatile pull-thru, cabin, and tent sites. Nature lovers will appreciate the walking trail, and creek access for canoeing, kayaking, and fishing.
History
buffs will appreciate its proximity to
Historic Harmony
, just a short walk across a footbridge, adding a cultural dimension to this scenic camping experience.
6. Pine Cove Beach Club & RV Resort
Address:
1495 Route 481, Charleroi, PA 15022
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~30 miles
Experience luxury at
Pine Cove Beach Club & RV Resort
with 38 deluxe RV camping sites surrounded by recreation options including ping pong, a treehouse, and swings. Spend the day
fishing
at the tranquil catch-and-release fishing lakes, teeming with a thriving bass population.
7. Hart’s Content Campground
Address:
496 Glendale Road, Beaver Falls, PA 15010
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~35 miles
Situated just half a mile from
Brush Creek Park
,
Hart’s Content Campground
offers a delightful camping experience with fire rings and picnic tables at every site. This accessible campground features a playground, a swimming pool with a handicap lift, beanbag and horseshoe courts.
8. Fox Den Acres Campground
Address:
390 Wilson Fox Rd, New Stanton, PA 15672
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~37 miles
Fox Den Acres Campground
provides a diverse range of amenities to guests, including a refreshing swimming pool, a captivating Wildlife Museum, and a well-stocked Trading Post with groceries and RV supplies. Positioned between
Pittsburgh's architectural gems
and the iconic
Fallingwater by Frank Lloyd Wright
, this campsite allows enthusiasts to seamlessly experience the rich architectural heritage of both destinations.
9. Smith Grove Campground
Address:
1085 Herman Rd, Butler, PA 16002
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~40 miles
Smith Grove Campground
, located north of the city and in close proximity to
Todd Nature Reserve
, provides a range of camping options. Whether you prefer rustic tent campsites or fully equipped cabins, or opt for the convenience of back-in or pull-through sites with full hook-ups, this rv campground caters to diverse preferences for a comfortable stay.
10. The Blue Canoe RV Resort
Address:
1101 Riveredge Rd, Connellsville, PA 15425
Distance to Pittsburgh:
~53 miles
Enjoy a perfect blend of relaxation and recreation at the tent and RV sites, and cabins at
The Blue Canoe RV Resort
. Adventurous guests can explore the water with kayaks, tubes, and tube trackers, while bike and eBike rentals provide a fun way to navigate the surroundings.
