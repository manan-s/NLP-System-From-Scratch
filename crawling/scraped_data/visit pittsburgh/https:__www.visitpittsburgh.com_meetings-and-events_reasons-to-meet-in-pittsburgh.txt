Discover the many reasons to host your event in Pittsburgh.
Pittsburgh is a must-see destination full of unique venues, attractions, restaurants and activities that are sure to wow your attendees. Add to that the iconic
David L. Lawrence Convention Center
, an incredible array of accommodations and an engaged hospitality community and services team, and you’ve got the ideal meeting destination.

Reasons to Meet Here
Connections
Pittsburgh is filled with innovative thought leaders and industry champions. From
key markets segments
in the region including technology, manufacturing, healthcare, education and everything in between, our team can make the connections you need for engaging speakers and community leaders to help you deliver ground-breaking meetings.
Hotels and Unique Venues
Whether you’re hosting a small gathering or a city-wide event, Pittsburgh has a wide array of
accommodations
and
meeting venues
to satisfy your need. Our 1.5 million-square-foot, Platinum LEED®-Certified
David L. Lawrence Convention Center
is a point of pride.

Things to Do and Dining
Your attendees will find this array of distinct
attractions
,
restaurants
and
activities
. Discover magnificent museums like The Andy Warhol Museum, fun-filled events like the award-winning
Picklesburgh
, historic sites like
Saint Anthony Chapel
, which is home to a record 5,000 Catholic relics, and an
arts & culture scene
unlike any other.
Convenient Travel
Pittsburgh
convenient location
makes for less than six hours of travel by car or train from Washington, D.C., Canada, and 10 other states. For those interested in flying, the award-winning
Pittsburgh International Airport
serves more than 8 million passengers annually, with nonstop destinations continually on the rise.
Sustainable Innovation
Once known for manufacturing, Pittsburgh is now a hub for technology, medicine, and education. Come see why industry giants like Facebook, Google and Uber, as well as home-grown tech companies like Duolingo and Argo AI are choosing to land in Pittsburgh.
With the shift in industries, Pittsburgh also became a sustainability focused city and a top-10 city for
certified green building space
.
Downtown Vitality
IndexPGH
is a new and one-of-its-kind digital dashboard that provides monthly updates on the well-being and vitality of downtown Pittsburgh – the heart of our entire 10-county region and an important economic center, with authentic and transparent data collected by the Allegheny Conference.
