Museums
From history, art, science, sports and more, Pittsburgh museums offer much to be explored.
If you love art, culture, and learning, Pittsburgh is a great place to explore. With our wide range of museums and galleries, there are endless must-do items to add to your agenda.

Whether you're looking for rainy day activities or seeking
family-friendly things to do
, Pittsburgh's museums offer diverse perspectives, engaging exhibits, and interactive galleries. Our museums feature a variety of indoor fun during the
colder months
. There are no shortage of galleries or exhibits for
sports lovers
,
foodies
, history experts, science enthusiasts, and
art connoisseurs
.

Uncover Multiple Layers of History
Plan for a history-filled itinerary in Pittsburgh, where fascinating treasures and artifacts await. Make your way to
Heinz History Center
, which features a wide range of Smithsonian objects, as well as an exhibits about the Underground Railroad, Mister Rogers, and Pittsburgh's contributions to American history. Dinosaur aficionados and geology buffs will enjoy spotting one-of-a-kind specimens and gems at
Carnegie Museum of Natural History
. Don't miss making a stop at
Fort Pitt Museum
, discovering Western Pennsylvania's impact on a historic events such as the American Revolution and the development of Pittsburgh.
Carnegie Museum of Natural History
Channel Your Curiosity on Your Own Scientific Adventure
Enrich your learning about the most awe-inspiring milestones in the scientific world with hands-on experiences and multifaceted exhibits. Visit
Moonshot Museum
for out-of-this-world memories, watching the assembly of spacecraft and participating in simulated lunar missions. Discover a wide range of eureka-like moments at
Daniel G. and Carole L. Kamin Science Center
, wandering through a planetarium, a submarine, and a fitness area where you can transform into a human yo-yo. Younger audiences will relish exciting activities at
Children's Museum of Pittsburgh
, where creativity is inspired by spots such as a waterplay area and a "Makeshop."
Revisit Iconic Pittsburgh Sports Moments
Pittsburgh sports fans will be enthralled by
Steelers Hall of Honor Museum
, which commemorates the history of the team with rare memorabilia and celebrates 49 inductees. For a more eclectic spin on your museum outing, visit
Bicycle Heaven Museum
, embarking on an adventure through the world's biggest bike museum and store.
