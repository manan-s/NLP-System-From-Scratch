This annual festival showcases Downtown Pittsburgh and is designed to have broad appeal across the Greater Pittsburgh community including young adults, suburban families, and small-town citizens. Picklesburgh™ is brought to you by
The Pittsburgh Downtown Partnership (PDP)
, the non-profit organization behind some of Pittsburgh’s most successful, popular, and imaginative events including;
Highmark Light Up Night®
, the Market Square Farmers Market, Friday & Saturday Night Markets, and the
Peoples Gas Holiday Market™
among many others.
