The Frick Pittsburgh Landscape App
The Frick Pittsburgh mobile app is the key companion for your visit to the museum and is available for FREE on the iTunes and Android store.
Download for iPhone
Download for Android
The app features an interactive map of the grounds that pinpoints your location and highlights selected buildings and points of interest. Let history come to life in the palm of your hands as you experience an augmented reality featuring images from the site at the turn of the 20th century
Features:
Pinpoint your location to help you navigate the Frick Museums and Gardens through GPS technology
Discover nearby augmented reality experiences (12 locations that feature a historic overlay of archival images using your phones camera function)
Identify and learn more about key trees and plants on site (30 species identified)
Experience the evolution of the site from the late 19th century to the present through the historic map feature
Learn about the history of the Frick through audio recordings of historic letters and documents
Save your favorites and share your experience on social media
Keep up to date with news and events from The Frick Pittsburgh newsletter.
