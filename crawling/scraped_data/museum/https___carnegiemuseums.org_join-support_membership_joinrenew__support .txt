Become a Member
Members explore more at Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh. Enjoy unlimited free admission to four world-class museums. Members receive great discounts, exclusive access, special events and more. Plus, your membership support ensures that our community continues to experience the treasures of the four Carnegie Museums.
Membership Levels
All members enjoy Membership Benefits:
FREE general admission for one year to Carnegie Museum of Art, Carnegie Museum of Natural History, Carnegie Science Center (including Highmark SportsWorks®), and The Andy Warhol Museum
Invitations to members-only events and programming
Free reciprocal admission at 300+ ASTC museums and science centers
Discounts on classes, camps, education films at The Rangos Giant Cinema, laser shows, lectures, and special events
A 10% discount in museum stores and cafes (20% store discount during Member Shopping Days)
Free subscription to

Free Carnegie Kid’s Club card, valid for one prize per month at the museum stores

How about giving the gift of membership?
It’s a great way to share with others the fun and inspiration of the four Carnegie Museums.

Support the museums, support the community.
A gift to Carnegie Museums reaches the community in so many rewarding ways. Join us as we help teach and inspire our region’s school children. Help us bring exhibitions to the region that only Carnegie Museums could bring—the kinds of exhibitions that engage people from all walks in life in the wonders of science, the powerful reach of art in all its forms, the joy of discovery.
