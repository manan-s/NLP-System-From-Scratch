The Andy Warhol Museum
Whether you’re planning an informal dinner for 20, a black-tie awards ceremony for 100, or a cocktail reception for 200, consider hosting your event at the home of all things Andy, including his many celebrity friends. All events have the option to add gallery floors, guided tours, and studio activities.
We offer an inclusive planning experience and our rental costs include all of the following:
Tables and chairs–highboy and low cocktail tables, larger round tables, lounge furniture, bar stools and chairs
Basic audio visual–house sound and lighting, projection capabilities, wireless microphones, and an audio visual tech
All security, custodial services, and special-events supervision.
Food and beverage is provided by our on-site exclusive caterer, and we will work with you directly to plan your menu.
Tell us about your event and let us get back to you with ideas.
