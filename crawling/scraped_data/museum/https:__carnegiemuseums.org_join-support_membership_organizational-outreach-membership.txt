Membership for Our Community Partners
Carnegie Museums is eager to welcome organizational partners as members, free-of-charge, so they can share the joy and wonder of the four Carnegie Museums with the individuals and families they serve.
Our complimentary Organizational Outreach Membership is available to qualifying 501(C)(3) community-based organizations that serve underserved youth, unhoused individuals, veterans, and seniors receiving public
assistance. When visiting, there must be at least one organizational staff member present with valid employment credentials, and the staff member must be accompanied by individuals served by the organization.
Each visit allows for a total of 11 people per day to any of the four Carnegie Museums. (Note: not for the personal use of an organization’s staff.)
Organizational Outreach Membership Benefits:
+
Unlimited FREE general admission to Carnegie Museums of Art and Natural History, Carnegie Science Center, and The Andy Warhol Museum for at least 1 organizational staff member and up to 10 individuals that the nonprofit serves
+
Invitations to events and programming designed with a teen-centric lens
+
Free reciprocal admission at 300+ ASTC museums and science centers
+
Discounts on classes, camps, educational films at the Rangos Giant Cinema, laser shows, lectures, and special events
+
A 10% discount in museum stores and cafes (20% store discount during Member Shopping Days)
+
Free subscription to
Carnegie
magazine
+
Free reciprocal admission at an additional 250+ ROAM museums and arboretums, plus 20+ major art and natural history museums
+
10 Half-off coupons for educational films at The Rangos Giant Cinema
