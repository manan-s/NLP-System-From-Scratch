Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh presents Hispanic Heritage Celebration
Saturday, Sept. 14
11 a.m.–3 p.m.
at Carnegie Museums of Art and Natural History
Free with museum admission
Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh is thrilled to honor and celebrate Hispanic Heritage Month at Carnegie Museums of Art and Natural History. Join us throughout the day for Latin music performances and dance classes with Los Sabrosos, La Palapa food truck, Spanish-language tours, and other activities throughout the museums.
Don’t miss our special activities!
11:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.:
La Palapa
food truck in the Sculpture Court.
11:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.:
Latin music performances and dance classes with
Los Sabrosos
.
Noon-2:00 p.m.:
Drop in dance instruction with Los Sabrosos.
11:30 and 12:30 p.m.:
Spanish Interpreted Tours at Carnegie Museum of Natural History
1:00 p.m.:
Spanish Interpreted Tours at Carnegie Museum of Art
