RESOURCES
Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh is the largest and most far-reaching cultural organization in Pittsburgh. Established in 1895 by Andrew Carnegie, Carnegie Museums is a collection of four distinctive museums: Carnegie Museum of Art, Carnegie Museum of Natural History, Carnegie Science Center, and The Andy Warhol Museum. We reach more than 1.4 million people a year through exhibitions, educational programs, outreach activities, and special events.
