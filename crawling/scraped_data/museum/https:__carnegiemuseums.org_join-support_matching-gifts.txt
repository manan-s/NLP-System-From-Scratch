Matching Gifts
Many employers sponsor matching gift programs and will match any charitable contributions or volunteer hours made by their employees. Some companies even match gifts made by retirees and/or spouses.
Follow the steps provided if your company is eligible. And, thank you! The impact of your gift to Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh may be doubled or possibly tripled!
