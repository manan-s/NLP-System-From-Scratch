How to Redeem a Passbook
1. Go to the login page at
www.pittsburghsymphony.org/myprofile
.
2. Enter your login name and password.  Your login name is your e-mail address.
- If you know your e-mail address, but forgot your password, go to
pittsburghsymphony.org/forgot_password/new
and enter your e-mail address.  An e-mail will be sent to that address with instructions for resetting your password.
3. Click on the name of the passbook you purchased.
4. The next page will show you how many tickets you have available to redeem in your passbook. Select the number of tickets you would like to redeem and accept the terms and conditions, then click "
Redeem".
5 The following screen is a listing of the performances for which you can redeem the tickets.  Select your concert and your seating choice (Select Your Own Seat or Best Available Seating). You will then select a performance date.
If you are
selecting your own seat
, you will have to select an area of the hall and a seat.
If you are using the
best available seating
, select the date and the section where you would like to sit.
6) The next screen is a summary of your purchase.  Select “
Proceed to Checkout
” to continue.
7) The pages that follow will take you through the checkout process.
If you have any problems with redeeming your passbook, please contact the box office at 412.392.4900.
