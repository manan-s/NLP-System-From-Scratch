Questions about Tickets, Orders, and other Customer Service Issues
If you have questions about ticket availability, lost tickets, ordering problems, delivery issues, or other questions or concerns, please call our Subscriber and Ticket Services department at 412-392-4900.
If you have a question about print at home tickets, please visit the
print at home FAQ page
.
If you would like to send us an e-mail, you may direct it to
customerservice@pittsburghsymphony.org
E-mail correspondence is handled at a lower priority than phone calls. If you have a concern or question that needs a timely response, please call the Subscriber and Ticket Services line at 412-392-4900. A reply to your e-mail may take several days.
If you're interested in a ticket donation for an event, visit out
ticket donation page
. Donation requests are not accepted through phone or mail.
General questions, comments and suggestions:
questions@pittsburghsymphony.org
Website Issues Only
