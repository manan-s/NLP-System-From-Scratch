Pittsburgh Opera proudly presents our 2024-25 Resident Artists.
These stellar artists were hand-selected after a grueling and competitive application and
audition process
.
During the course of the 2024-25 Season, these talented individuals will perform:
The principal and supporting roles in the fully-staged Pittsburgh Opera productions
Supporting roles, covering, and sometimes principal roles in Pittsburgh Opera's mainstage productions
In programs for public schools, community centers, libraries and corporate events
In regular Song Shop concerts, free programs, and special events
At parties hosted by Pittsburgh Opera
