New Guard Facebook Group
.

BENEFITS
The benefits you'll receive with your yearly New Guard Membership are:
Two complimentary tickets (with an individual membership) or four complimentary tickets (with a dual membership) to an upcoming performance of your choice, seated at best availability subject to performance availability.
Upon choosing performance dates for comp tickets, allow a few days for processing.
You'll be added to our Pittsburgh Opera New Guard E-Newsletter to stay up to date on all New Guard happenings.
20% discount on additional tickets
Special rates to Pittsburgh Opera Special Events
Free admission to all New Guard Events
Admission and special promotions for collaborative events with other Pittsburgh young professional organizations
Networking opportunities
PURCHASE A MEMBERSHIP

$75 for an individual membership with 2 complimentary tickets

NEW GUARD + GALAXY SOCIETY
This season, New Guard members can join Pittsburgh Opera’s
Galaxy Society
at a reduced rate. Enjoy networking and complimentary hospitality in the donor lounge, white glove ticket service, special behind-the-scenes invitations and more.
Benefits:
Donor Lounge networking and hospitality at the Benedum Center and other select venues for you and a guest
Exclusive entry to Opening Night Cast Parties
Ticket concierge service for access to the best seats in the house
Complimentary parking for Benedum Center and August Wilson Center performances
Invitations to all Sitzprobe with a reception featuring wine, cheese, and socializing
Admission to all Dress Rehearsals
Complimentary admission to Art Song Recitals and Opera Up Close
Name recognition in Pittsburgh Opera program books
Galaxy Society membership is regularly priced at $3,500, but New Guard members can join with a gift of just $2,000.
Questions? Contact
Victoria Touscany
.

Recurring New Guard Events
Summer Salon Happy Hour
– Join us on
Thursday, September 19
! Get a jump on the new opera season with all your New Guard friends! The Summer Salon features a live performance from our Resident Artists, free food, plus a free drink ticket for New Guard members.
Opening Night Happy Hours
– Celebrate each Benedum Center opera’s opening night with a pre-show happy hours, complete with food discounts and custom-cocktails created to tie into the production. The 2024-25 happy hours with be on October 5 (
Tosca
), November 9 (
Cavalleria Rusticana/Pagliacci
) and March 22 (LGBTQ+ Ally Night at
Madama Butterfly
.

LGBTQ+/Ally Night
– Join us every year on opening night of our first Benedum spring opera to raise a glass to a good cause. With your discounted ticket, enjoy pre-show drinks and intermissions in a private lounge at the Benedum. A portion of each ticket sale for this event supports a local LGBTQ+ organization.
