88 Keys for Pittsburgh
Thank you to our important supporters who helped fund the purchase of the PSO’s new Steinway concert piano making its debut during the 2022-2023 season!
Between 2018 and 2020 the
88 Keys for Pittsburgh
Campaign raised over $200,000 from more than 200 households and organizations to purchase our new Steinway concert piano.
We wish to recognize the
88 Keys for Pittsburgh
Committee, the many volunteers who gave their time and talent to the project, and to the donors listed below for their financial support.
Thank You!
