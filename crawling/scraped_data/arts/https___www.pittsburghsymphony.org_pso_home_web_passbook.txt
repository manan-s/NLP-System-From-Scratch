About Passbooks
Passbook vouchers give you discounted seats with the ability to schedule concerts at your convenience. Each Passbook voucher can be redeemed for one ticket to a BNY Mellon Grand Classics, PNC Pops, Highmark Holiday Pops, or most special performances of the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra at Heinz Hall.
How to Order & Redeem
To order your Passbook, select from the number of vouchers above. Your Passbook will arrive in 7-10 days with instructions for choosing your shows and seats. Seating is subject to availability and certain restrictions apply. One voucher can be exchanged for one ticket.
Giving as a gift? Free gift envelopes are available. Simply indicate the number of gift envelopes desired in the "Special Instructions" box when ordering.
