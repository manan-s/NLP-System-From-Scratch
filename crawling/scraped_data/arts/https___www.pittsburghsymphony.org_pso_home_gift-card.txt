Passbooks
Passbook vouchers give you discounted seats with the ability to schedule concerts at your convenience. Each Passbook voucher can be redeemed for one ticket to a BNY Mellon Grand Classics, PNC Pops, Highmark Holiday Pops, or most special performances of the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra at Heinz Hall.
