Accessibility
The Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra and Heinz Hall are committed to providing an environment that is inclusive and welcoming to all patrons.
Below, please find a list of our accessible services. If you should have further questions, please call 412.392.4900. Doormen and ushers are also available for assistance at every performance.
Accessible Services
Curbside Assistance
Accessible Entrances
Accessible Seating Locations with Companion Seats
If a wheelchair location is desired, please notify the ticket service representative at the time of purchase. This will help us ensure that the fixed seat can be removed prior to your arrival at the theater.
Accessible Restroom
Dauler Hearing Loop
Click
here
for more information about our
Dauler Hearing Loop
for hearing aids or cochlear implants.
Guide Dogs
Guide dogs are gladly accommodated at all events. Please inquire when buying tickets.
Braille Programs
Braille programs are available at the concierge desk for all BNY Classics and PNC Pops performances.
Large Print
Large print photo copies of all BNY Mellon Grand Classics, PNC Pops, and Fiddlesticks Family Series program books are available at the concierge desk.
Portable Assistive Listening
Devices are available at all events. Please ask an usher for assistance when you arrive.
Sign Language
ASL interpretation is available for any event upon request.
Sensory-friendly performances are scheduled occasionally.
If you have specific questions, please ask your ticketing representative or call Heinz Hall at 412.392.4900.
