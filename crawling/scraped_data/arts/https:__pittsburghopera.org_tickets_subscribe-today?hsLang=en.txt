SUBSCRIBER BENEFITS
​We Love Our Subscribers!
Great benefits include:
The best seats at the best prices
Easy, flexible, FREE ticket exchanges
Early access to single tickets and a 15% discount on additional single tickets to mainstage shows
20% discount on tickets to second-stage productions at CAPA Theater and the Bitz Opera Factory
Reserved parking at Theater Square Garage for $15 per performance

: Be delighted with your first opera, or we will refund the rest of your subscription.
