Cheap Seats
Cheap Seats is the easy and affordable way for students, faculty, and staff at our partner colleges to enjoy great opera. Discounts range from up to
60% off for students
and up to
50% off for faculty and staff
for select seating in our most popular seating areas. All you need is a valid college ID!
For University of Pittsburgh students, we proudly partner with Pitt Arts. In addition to online ordering, members of the Pitt community can go to Pitt Arts at the William Pitt Union, and fill out a Cheap Seats order form. For more information visit
Pitt Arts
.

Wendy Parkulo
