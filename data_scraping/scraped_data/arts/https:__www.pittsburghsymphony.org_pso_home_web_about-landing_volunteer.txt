Jack Heinz Advisory Board
The purpose of the Jack Heinz Society is to identify, cultivate and engage corporate and community leadership in Pittsburgh Symphony activities to create advocates throughout the community.
Jack Heinz Next Gen
Jack Heinz Next Gen is an entry point for greater involvement with the Pittsburgh Symphony. Members learn about the Symphony and promote awareness of and involvement with the PSO among their communities.
Pittsburgh Symphony Association
The Pittsburgh Symphony Association is a volunteer organization committed to the support of the Pittsburgh Symphony Orchestra through financial, educational and promotional activities.
