Student matinee of The Marriage of Figaro
University students, faculty, and staff can save money on Pittsburgh Opera tickets with
Cheap Seats
,
Student Rush, or
Group Tickets
. University students can also get involved as a
Pittsburgh Opera student ambassador
.
Cheap Seats
Cheap Seats is the easy and affordable way for students, faculty, and staff at our partner colleges and universities to enjoy great opera. Use the
discount codes
for your school to receive the special discounted rate.
Student Rush – Half Price Seats!
Starting
2 hours
before every performance, students with valid school ID can go to the Benedum Center Box Office and purchase up to two tickets for any available seats for half price.
Get a Group Together for Special Discounts
Groups of 6 or more students, faculty, and staff can get great group prices and all the benefits of traditional groups. Contact
Wendy Parkulo
for more information.
Other Student Discounts
Students in grades K-12 are eligible for half-price children's tickets.
