FRIENDS
of Pittsburgh Opera
FRIENDS
of Pittsburgh Opera (FPO) is both a friend-raising and fund-raising body in support of Pittsburgh Opera.
Membership in
FRIENDS
of Pittsburgh Opera is $75 per household of 2 or more, or $50 per individual. Your membership fee supports Pittsburgh Opera financially, and your participation helps promote interest in, and awareness of, Pittsburgh Opera in the community.
FPO membership benefits include:
Invitations to community events throughout the season
Invitations to fabulous social events like the Holiday Party and the Garden Party
Free admission to
Opera Up Close
and
Art Song Recitals
Opportunities to volunteer and meet new friends
