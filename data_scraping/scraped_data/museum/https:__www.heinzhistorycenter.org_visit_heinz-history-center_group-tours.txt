Group Tours
The Heinz History Center welcomes adult group tours by offering discounted admission rates for groups of 10 or more. Docent-guided tours are available by reservation and last between 60 and 90 minutes. Reservations for docent guided tours must be made at least four weeks in advance. Self-guided tours should be booked at minimum one week in advance.
Adult Group Admission Prices (10 or more people):
Self-Guided Visit: $12 per person
Docent-Guided Tour: $14 per person guided
When you’re ready to book, or if you have any questions, please contact our Group Tours team via email at
