Carnegie Museums is a Family Affair
There’s nothing like family time together at the four Carnegie Museums. Access to our museum exhibitions, collections, educational experiences, and other programming can be transformative for people of all ages. That’s why Carnegie Museums is helping to remove barriers to membership with a $20 Family Access Membership for individuals and families who live in Pennsylvania and are eligible to receive public assistance.
Please join the Carnegie Museums member family!
Family-level membership benefits include:
+
FREE general admission for 2 adults + 4 children for one year to Carnegie Museum of Art, Carnegie Museum of Natural History, Carnegie Science Center, and The Andy Warhol Museum
+
Exclusive access and invitations to members-only events
+
FREE reciprocal admission at 300+ ASTC museums and science centers PLUS 250+ ROAM museums and arboretums
+
Discounts on classes, camps, educational films at The Rangos Giant Cinema, laser shows, lectures, and special events
+
A 10% discount in museum stores and cafes (20% store discount during Member Shopping Days)
+
FREE subscription to
Carnegie
magazine

*To qualify for the ACCESS discount, you must be receiving or eligible to receive benefits or assistance in the state of Pennsylvania. ACCESS is applicable only to the individual applying and cannot be applied to other parties. ACCESS cannot be purchased on behalf of the qualifying party or as a gift for a qualifying party. Carnegie Museums of Pittsburgh reserves the right to cancel any Teen Membership, Family Access Membership or Organizational Access Membership should an applicant submit incomplete or false information.
