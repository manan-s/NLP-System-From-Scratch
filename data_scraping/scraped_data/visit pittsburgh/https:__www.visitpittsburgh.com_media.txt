Media
From press releases to photo requests here's all the information you'll need on media about Pittsburgh.
VisitPittsburgh's Online News Room is a centralized
information resource
for all news media. Our
Communications staff
gladly assists qualified travel, trade and news journalists with a number of helpful services including scheduling interviews; organizing small-group
press tours
; arranging itineraries and facility visits to arts + cultural institutions, hotels and restaurants, and select tours and events. The team also provides fact-checking services and background information on Pittsburgh, as well as ideas for travel-related stories or information related to convention + meeting services and trends.
Press Kit
The Online Press Kit is a resource for journalists interested in experiencing and writing about the Pittsburgh region. It contains a bevy of information, including new developments, story ideas and news releases.
