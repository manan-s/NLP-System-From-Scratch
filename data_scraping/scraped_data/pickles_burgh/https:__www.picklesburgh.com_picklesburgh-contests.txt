Picklesburgh Contests
Congratulations to Fred Smith of Erie, Pennsylvania for taking home the crown in the 2017 Picklesburgh Pickle Juice Drinking Contest! Fred held off 49 other competitors to be named “The Mayor of Picklesburgh.” Smith was the fastest contestant to drink 32 ounces of cold pickle juice at just 8.34 seconds.
